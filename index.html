I’m Kyle Jones - a passionate and enthusiastic gameplay and tools engineer who loves to design and develop game-focused applications!

BACKGROUND

Based in the East Midlands, UK, I completed a BSc Psychology from the University of Derby in 2018, but later decided I wanted to go down a separate career path. I’ve played video games for as long as I can remember, so when it came to deciding on that career path, it’s a given that I was drawn to game development. I think every gamer has at least once in their life thought “how hard can it be?” - which, looking back on it, is a very naive thought...

I started my development journey with C++ and I found that, unlike most things, even learning the basics of programming was fun. Once I felt that I knew enough to start working on a project of my own, I fell in love with the idea of creating my own game engine to hone my skills instead of the typical beginner projects that everyone recommends that you start with.

This was of course a much more arduous task than I originally anticipated and is something that I’m still working on to this day, but I believe that it’s given me a much more solid foundation than if I were to just make a Tic-Tac-Toe game like everyone online recommends, even more so because my educational background is not in Computer Science.



I’m open to any and all development roles, so please do get in touch at kyjo1308@gmail.com!

PROJECTS

Apostle Engine

The Apostle Engine is an in-development 2D/3D game engine. The engine currently supports Windows and is exclusively using OpenGL as it's graphics API, with plans to eventually transition to using Vulkan.

Apostle is desinged to be a lightweight, multi-platform tool for small teams to be able to work with efficiently.

UsefulUtils

UsefulUtils is a Unity package containing features and functionality that is useful in most Unity projects, such as serialization, math functions, and more.
